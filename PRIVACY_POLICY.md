# LeadPad Privacy Policy

_Last updated: [DATE — fill in before publishing]_

## What LeadPad does

LeadPad is a Chrome extension that lets you save publicly visible LinkedIn
profile information (name, headline, company, location, and profile URL)
that you choose to save while browsing LinkedIn as a signed-in user, and
export it as a CSV file.

## What data we collect

- **Profile data you explicitly save**: name, job title, company, location,
  and profile URL of LinkedIn profiles you click "Save lead" on. This is
  stored locally in your browser (`chrome.storage.local`) — **it is not
  sent to LeadPad's servers or any third party.**
- **License key** (Premium users only): the license key you enter to
  activate Premium is sent to our payment provider (Gumroad, or our own
  license server if you're on the Stripe plan) solely to verify it's valid.
  We don't store your key on any server we control beyond what's needed to
  verify your purchase.
- **We do not collect**: browsing history, LinkedIn messages, connection
  requests, passwords, or any data beyond what you explicitly save as a
  lead.

## What we don't do

- We do not sell, rent, or share your saved lead data with anyone.
- We do not automate LinkedIn actions (connection requests, messages,
  automated scrolling/searching) — LeadPad only reads what's already
  rendered on the page for you as a logged-in user.
- We do not track you across other websites.

## Data storage and deletion

All saved leads live in your browser's local storage and stay on your
device. You can delete them anytime via the "Clear all" button in the
extension popup, or by uninstalling the extension (which removes all
locally stored data).

## Third-party services

- **Gumroad / Stripe**: used only to process payments and verify license
  keys. See their respective privacy policies:
  [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/privacy) /
  [Stripe](https://stripe.com/privacy).
- **LinkedIn**: LeadPad runs on linkedin.com pages but is not affiliated
  with, endorsed by, or connected to LinkedIn Corporation.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Material changes will
be reflected here with an updated date.
